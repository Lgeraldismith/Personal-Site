Key Takeaways

  • Creative Expression in Technical Fields

    Engineering coursework often lacks creative outlets, making multimodal composition a valuable space for expressing technical knowledge in engaging, creative ways.

  • Personal Experience as Rhetorical Tool

    Drawing from personal experiences with video games, escapism, and academic stress creates authentic work that resonates with audiences and effectively conveys complex issues.

  • Growth Through Iteration

    The progression from comic strip to zine demonstrates how applying rhetorical techniques learned in class deepens the effectiveness and emotional impact of creative work.

  • Bridging Passion and Pedagogy

    Combining interests like video game lore and touchscreen engineering with multimodal composition creates work that educates while exploring both tangible and intangible concepts.

Final Thoughts

"This course gave me the rhetorical tools necessary to create work that effectively illustrates my experience and actually makes the reader feel the issue. The joy of having a creative outlet carried through to every piece I created."
