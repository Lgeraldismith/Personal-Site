Content Creator Inspiration

To inform my approach to science communication and educational content, I analyzed the work of three prominent educational content creators:

  • Veritasium - Known for engaging science explanations and thought-provoking experiments
  • Vsauce - Master of building curiosity through philosophical questions about everyday phenomena
  • 3Blue1Brown - Creates visually stunning mathematical explanations through elegant animations

These creators demonstrate how combining clear narration, strong visual aids, and strategic pacing can make technical topics both understandable and compelling. Their approaches influenced my decision to use visual demonstrations and relatable examples to explain touchscreen technology.
