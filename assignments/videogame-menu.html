Assignment 05

Videogame Menu

UI/UX design and interactive visual communication

Mode: Visual, Spatial, Gestural
Format: UI/UX Design

Introduction

For this assignment, I created Gustave's Journal—a videogame menu that functions as an in-world bestiary for a fictional game. This project explores how UI/UX design can enhance narrative immersion by presenting game mechanics through the lens of world-building and character perspective.

The Concept: "For Those Who Come After"

I have created Gustave's journal as a bestiary, featuring a weathered cover and two monster entries: the Évêque and the Serpenphare. As for the narrative, Expeditioners are taught to record their travels and journeys for use by the next expedition. They have a phrase, "For those who come after", that emphasizes their philosophy of leaving a legacy of knowledge. A bestiary in the form of a journal is exactly what might help future expeditions in their journey to take down The Paintress.

Design Inspiration

The bestiary design draws inspiration from Monster Hunter's creature documentation system, particularly the Rathalos entry format. This reference informed the visual layout, the balance between image and text, and the presentation of creature weaknesses—creating a familiar yet distinct interface that honors established gaming conventions while serving the unique narrative of Gustave's world.
