Assignment 06

Webcomic Strip

Sequential art and visual storytelling

Mode: Visual, Linguistic, Spatial
Format: Sequential Art

Project Overview

This webcomic strip explores the universal student experience of struggling through a challenging class, using humor and visual storytelling to capture the relatable moment when complex concepts suddenly become clear after some rest.

"The Evolution of Understanding"

View full size image

From the Essay

Everyone who has ever been a student has at one point been bored in a classroom and "The Evolution of Understanding" cartoonifies that experience for humorous purposes. Humor evolves from the relatability of a boring class and the taboo nature of sleeping in said class.
